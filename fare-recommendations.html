Fare Tier Recommendations

Which fare to pick on Loganair and BA, what fits in the cabin on each airline, and how to pack your bags for every leg. Bottom line: Fly Flex+ on Loganair, Euro Traveller Plus on BA.

Loganair · Norwich → Aberdeen · Wed Jul 22

Book Fly Flex+ at £199.99

Skip the £257 "Fly Flex Plus Fair" — the extra £57 buys date-change flexibility you don't need (your dates are pinned). Fly Flex+ gives you 30 kg hold, fast-track security, missed-flight protection, and premium seating — all for just £25 more than the base Fly Flex.

⚠️ Critical: Loganair cabin is tiny. Only 6 kg / 40 × 35 × 18 cm allowed in the cabin. Your European-size Tumi roller won't fit — it goes in the hold. Your REI backpack becomes your one cabin bag on this flight. They weigh at the gate.
British Airways · Inverness → Heathrow T5 · Jul 28 or 29

Book Euro Traveller Plus

Do NOT book Basic — Basic has no checked bag and you'd pay £40–60 at the airport to add one. Plus is only ~£25–35 more and includes 1 × 23 kg checked bag, free same-day change, and seat selection. Plus Flex (unlimited changes, full refund) isn't worth the extra £60–100 since your Inverness dates are fixed.

❓ One question before you book Loganair:
Are you checking one bag (Euro-size Tumi only) or both Tumis on the Loganair flight?
  • One bag: Fly Flex+ at £199.99 — all set, no extra fee.
  • Both bags: Fly Flex+ at £199.99 + pre-paid 2nd hold bag (~£25–35). Total ~£225–235. Still cheaper than Fly Flex Plus Fair at £257.

Same question applies on BA out of Inverness — Plus includes 1 checked bag; a 2nd costs extra at booking (~£30–40, much cheaper than at the airport).
